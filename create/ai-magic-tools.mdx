## Text to music

"Text to music" feature enables users to type in a prompt, and the AI technology within Soundverse can generate the necessary audio content from that text input. This approach streamlines the content creation process and simplifies music composition, making it accessible to individuals who may not have extensive knowledge of music theory or complex software. User just need to type the prompt and click on the arrow button to generate audio. Writing a perfect prompt or explanatory prompt plays an essential role in generating perfect audios.
Here are some tips to draft the perfect prompt-

- Specify the genre- Rock, electronic, house, pop, jazz etc.
- Mention the instruments to be included- Guitar, Bass, Synth, Piano, Drums etc.
- Narrate the vibe of the audio to Soundverse-

Note: For better results mention the speed in bpm to assistant to get desired output.

- After receiving initial outputs, feel free to iterate and refine your prompt based on the results.
- Don't be afraid to let the AI surprise you. Sometimes, giving it some creative freedom can yield fantastic results.

**Example prompts-**
“In a packed arena, the electric guitar wailed and the drums thundered. The crowd surged with adrenaline as the rock band'senergetic performance ignited a rebellious spirit, setting the stage for a night of wild rock 'n' roll rebellion. Speed of the song is very fast!”

## Stem Separation

The stem separation feature allows users to separate the different elements of a song, such as vocals, drums, and bass. With stem separation you can sperate a mixed audio track into its constituent parts, or stems. It is a powerful tool for musicians and producers, as it allows them to isolate and manipulate individual tracks in a mix.

## Extend Audio

Soundverse's Extend Audio feature is a tool that empowers users to break free from the confines of fixed audio lengths. Whether extending the audios generated by Soundverse or uploading their own compositions, musicians can seamlessly experiment and refine their musical narratives. This user-friendly feature provides a dynamic canvas for creative exploration, fostering an environment where the length of a composition becomes a malleable element in the artist's toolkit, opening up endless possibilities for collaboration and sonic innovation. Users can extend seamlessly extend their audios in s single click. 

### Method 1 (Extend generated audio)

The first method to extend the audio is by extending the audio generated by Soundverse. You can hover over the generated audio, click the three dots and then select extend audio from the AI magic tool option available. Soundverse’s AI will process this and will generate the output automatically and you will get the desired output in a while. 

### Method 2 (Upload the Audio)

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    In the prompts bar, Click the three dots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
     Then under AI magic tools, select extend audio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
   From the dialog box shown select the audio from your system you wish to extend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
   Select the audio, then it will prompt that audio has been uploaded and a music icon will be shwon in the prompt bar.
  </Step>
   <Step title="Fifth Step">
   Press enter, sit back and relax till Soundverse extends audio for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
