## What is Soundverse?

With the help of Soundverse Assistant and AI magic tools, our users get an unfair advantage over other creators to create content easily and quickly.
Soundverse Assistant is your ultimate music companion. You simply speak to the assistant to get your stuff done. The more you speak to it, the more it starts understanding you and your goals. Simply put, they help convert your creative dreams into tangible music/audio.
Use AI Magic Tools such as Text to Music, Lyrics Writing or Stem Separation to realize your content dreams quicker.
