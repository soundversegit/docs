<Accordion title="Which plan is right for me?">
  Go to https://www.soundverse.ai/pricing, you will find both monthly and annual pricing options. We have four plans available - Free, Creator, Pro and Enterprise/ Custom. You can check out the features of each and every plan and get it for yourself. You can avail 20% discount if you choose annual plans.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I see which Tier I'm in?">
  You can simply go to Manage your plan option under profile options and it clearly shows available tokens and current plan.  
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I Upgrade or Cancel my plan?">
  You can simply go to Manage your plan option under profile options and Upgrade/ Cancel your plan from there.
</Accordion>
