<Accordion title="What is the pricing policy of Soundverse?">
  It is available for free trial with some features, you can upgrade the plan to access all the features. To know more about the pricing policy, visit https://www.soundverse.ai/pricing.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="If I start a monthly subscription, is it just for one month or will it renew automatically?">
 - Tokens renew every month.
 - As part of subscription, you'll get additional tokens added on top of remaining tokens every month. For e.f. if you had 1,200 tokens left- next month you'll have 2,600 + 1,200 tokens = 3,800 Tokens under Pro Tier.
 - For AppSumo users, your tokens will refresh to 1000 or 2600 tokens depending on Tier.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I buy additional tokens?">
   Simply navigate to "Manage Your Plan" under Profile Settings. Click on "Top Up Tokens" and slide to the number of tokens you'd want and click on "Buy Now". https://www.soundverse.ai/myaccount
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel my plan?">
 Simply navigate to "Manage Your Plan" under Profile Settings. Press Cancel and you'll taken to cancellation process.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What do I do if I accidentally selected a yearly plan instead of monthly?">
  You can always cancel the subscription at any point you want by following the steps mentioned. We'd also be happy to help you with the refund process.
  Make sure to reach out to us within 48 hours of this accident. 
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="If I upgrade from a lower plan to a higher plan, do I have to pay the full rate of the higher plan?">
  No, you just have to pay the remaining amount i.e. the difference amount of existing plan and the plan you are upgrading to.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Do you offer student or educator discounts?">
  We do not offer any student or educator discount as of now. We'd love to do it in the future.
  
  If you are an enterprise we can customize the plan for you. However, if you would like your school or institute to use our platform, then write to us to discuss further and get a deal.
</Accordion>
