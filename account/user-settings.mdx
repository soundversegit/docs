## My account

### General

In here, you can setup your profile picture, see by what means did you log in, set a username and save the changes. You even get a logout button in this screen to exit from Soundverse.

### Project Setting

You get the option to turn on privacy for your prompts/messages. You need to push the button to the right to make your prompts private. This works when you have upgraded to the paid plan of Soundverse otherwise it will prompt to upgrade to the paid-tier.

### Manage your plan

You will see your token balance and the status of your plan with Soundverse i.e. free, pro etc. You will also find an upgrade option here to get on a higher plan if available.

### Invite

You can invite your friends to Soundverse by giving them “INVITE CODE” from here. You will find a special invite code. You can invite upto 10 people using special invite code generated for each invite as invite code can be used by one person only.
