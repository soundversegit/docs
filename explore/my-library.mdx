## Liked Clips

You will find my library option on the left side of the studio, it has your liked clips and collections so far. It is your personal audio library section provided by Soundverse wherein you can find your liked audios form the projects which you have created in the Soundverse studio and collections in a click!

Liked clips: These are the generated audios which you have liked in the studio. Sometimes you may regenerate the audios to get the desired results and in order to save it for future use or to bookmark them you can simply press the heart/like button besides the play button of the audio to get it saved in liked clips in my library.
