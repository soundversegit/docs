We're thrilled to unveil the new Explore section in Soundverse! Discover and listen to prompts crafted by fellow users, and show your support by liking your favorites. This feature allows you to uncover fresh ideas and promote the work of talented creators. Simply navigate to the Explore tab in the right. 
Upon finding a prompt that piques your interest, click to listen and express your appreciation by liking it. Additionally, the Featured tab present under the explore section showcases top picks picked up by the algorithm.
